Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

How do I place pictures in just the place that I want?

Take advantage of the drag-and-drop interface and use keyboard-arrows to position photos anywhere on the video canvas.

What are ways of creating the picture-in-picture effects from photos?

Place and resize photos to make inset frames and crop photos for a professional picture-in-picture effect.

Can I make a photo slideshow while using background music?

Yes, join several photos and audio tracks into a video with the help of the video editor. Synchronise time to music beats to achieve professional outcomes.

Can I edit photos after inserting them to the video?

Adjust opacity, brightness, contrast, saturation, and hue settings for all or each of the photos as you add them to your video timeline, even after positioning them there.

What file formats can be used for photo overlays?

Vidolid can open various popular picture formats, such as JPG, PNG, GIF, and WEBP. For the base video, you can use MP4, MOV, and AVI.

Is it possible to set the time at which photos appear and disappear?

Choose the desired time of appearance and disappearance of overlays for each photo with the help of the timeline editor and set the precise moment in the image option panel.

How many pixels should a photo for video overlays have?

Use images that are resolution-matched or better than your video quality, usually 1920x1080pixels for HD videos.

How do I make collage effect from several photos?

Layout a collage of multiple photographs on screen at the same time, change the size and position of images.
